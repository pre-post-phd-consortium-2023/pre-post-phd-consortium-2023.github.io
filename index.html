Pre-PhD symposium and Post-PhD consortium

The IDC school of Design, IIT Bombay is happy to announce a two-day workshop for
a) designers who are interested in doing a PhD in the future, and
b) designers from India who have done PhD in the past 10 years (or are about to complete their PhD soon, including IDC PhD alumni)


Important dates

500-word statement of purpose due (for designers who are interested in doing a PhD): December 25, 2022

Notification of acceptance and feedback: December 30, 2022

Workshop: January 28 and 29, 2023

Contents

These will be a two-day event targeted to two groups of people. One group will be designers from India who have done a PhD within the past ten years or are about to complete their PhD soon (2013-2023), including IDC PhD alums. The second group will be designers (including IDC alums) across India who might be interested in doing a Design PhD in the future.

The event will invite eminent researchers in the domain, post-doctoral designers from India, and designers interested in doing a design PhD. The contents will include invited talks, experience reports, a showcase of IDC research, including both faculty members and students, and talks and panel discussions by the invitees.

There will be opportunities to explore specific areas of mutual interest and developing a proposal for a PhD. This event will be an opportunity for designers to interact with eminent researchers in the domain.

Should You attend?

You should attend this event if you are:

  • A practicing designer seeking growth in research
  • A designer, engineer, architect, or an artist with research interests in any of the topics discussed above.

This workshop is for those who are not yet sure if they should take a plunge into a 4 to 5 year commitment for a PhD. If it sounds like you are one or more of the above, this workshop will help. It is meant for those who are interested in design research, want to do a PhD, but need help to develop a concrete PhD proposal.

To Participate

To participate in the workshop, please send a 500-word statement of purpose (SoP) to 214138001[at the rate]iitb.ac.in by December 25, 2022. Your SoP should contain the following points:

  • Your educational and professional background
  • Your research interests

Applicants will be shortlisted based on this SoP and intimated by December 30, 2022.

Shortlisted applicants will have to pay a nominal registration fee of Rs. 1,000.

Lunch, tea and snacks will be provided during the workshop. Participants will have to make their own arrangements for travel. We have hostel provisions for participants' accommodation.
